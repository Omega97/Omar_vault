
# Red Flag Green Flag


---

## Game Rules

**Material:** A standard 54-card deck (52 French cards + 2 Jokers).

#### How to Play

1.  A player draws a single card from the deck.
    
2.  In silence, each player then secretly chooses a flag based on the trait associated with that card.
    * **Red Flag:** You consider the trait a negative.
    * **Green Flag:** You consider the trait a positive.
    
3.  All players reveal their chosen flags simultaneously, and discuss their choices.
