**Dōbutsu Shōgi** (or Animal Shogi) is a kid-friendly shogi variant played on a 3×4 grid. ==You take turns moving one animal piece per turn to capture the opponent's Lion, or safely advance your own Lion to the opponent's back row==. 

The Pieces & Their Moves

Each piece has dots printed on it that show which directions it can move: [

- **Lion:** Moves one space in any direction (like a King).
- **Giraffe:** Moves one space orthogonally (forward, backward, left, or right) .
- **Elephant:** Moves one space diagonally .
- **Chick:** Moves one space forward.
- **Hen:** Promoted from a Chick . It moves one space in any direction except diagonally backward . 

Gameplay Mechanics

- **Capturing:** When you land on a square occupied by an opponent's animal, you capture it . Captured pieces go into your "hand" and can be dropped back onto the board on your turn. 
- **Promotions:** If your Chick reaches the farthest row (your opponent's starting row), it promotes and flips over into a Hen . If a Hen is captured by your opponent, it reverts back to a Chick when placed into their hand. 
- **Drops:** Instead of moving a piece, you can drop any captured piece from your hand onto an empty space. Dropped Chicks cannot be promoted on the same turn they are dropped. 

How to Win

You win the game in two ways: [[1](https://shogi.cz/wp-content/uploads/dobutsuEN.pdf), [2](https://kids.kiddle.co/D%C5%8Dbutsu_sh%C5%8Dgi)]

1. **Catch the Lion:** Capture your opponent's Lion.
2. **Escape the Lion:** Successfully move your own Lion into the furthest promotion row, as long as it is not placed in immediate danger. [[1](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/D%C5%8Dbutsu_sh%C5%8Dgi), [2](https://shogi.cz/wp-content/uploads/dobutsuEN.pdf), [3](https://kids.kiddle.co/D%C5%8Dbutsu_sh%C5%8Dgi)]

Draws

If the exact same board position occurs four times in a row, the game ends in a draw (called "Thousand-Day moves" or perpetual check). [[1](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/D%C5%8Dbutsu_sh%C5%8Dgi), [2](https://shogi.cz/wp-content/uploads/goroEN.pdf), [3](https://kids.kiddle.co/D%C5%8Dbutsu_sh%C5%8Dgi)]
