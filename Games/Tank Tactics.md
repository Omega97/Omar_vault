## The game in practice

The players will participate in a dedicated **Telegram chat**. We will meet **once a day**, when all the living players are available. Players will submit the actions, and the *Game Master* will execute the actions in order.

At the beginning of the meeting, every live tank gets +1 AP, and the player actions are discussed by voice. To settle priority disputes, we shall rely on the group chat, standing by the motto: *"First come, first served!"*
