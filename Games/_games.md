# 🎮 Games & Game Design

Large collection of game design documents, card/board game prototypes, and full systems (many imported/expanded).

---

## RPG & Narrative Systems

Complete tabletop RPG systems.

- [New Beginning - Manuale ⚪️](RPG%20Systems/New%20Beginning%20-%20Manuale%20⚪️.md) – Lightweight narrative RPG: risk via drawing white/black tokens from a bag, character sheets with traits/lessons/sventures, evolution, group interactions, and narrator guidance. Also known in context as Village Horizons 🏝.

## Party & Social Games

Group games built around discussion, debate, deduction, and social interaction.

- [Battle Bro-yale ⭐️](Party%20Games/Battle%20Bro-yale%20⭐️.md) – Category showdown elimination: two players face off in a random category while the rest vote on the winner.
- [Hear me Out 🎙️](Party%20Games/Hear%20me%20Out%20🎙️.md) – Debate game: defend assigned opinions and earn points for convincing arguments.
- [Red Flag Green Flag 🚩](Party%20Games/Red%20Flag%20Green%20Flag%20🚩.md) – Trait-reaction game: secretly pick red or green flags on drawn cards, then discuss.
- [The Dream Team 🚀](Party%20Games/The%20Dream%20Team%20🚀.md) – Team-selection social game: predict the Capo's picks and justify your candidacy.
- [Information Economy - The Key 🗝](Party%20Games/Information%20Economy%20-%20The%20Key%20🗝.md) – Deduction party game: trade information, bluff, and identify the hidden Key Card.

## Card Games

### Poker & Betting

- [Poker-32](Card%20Games/Poker%20%26%20Betting/Poker-32.md) – Simplified heads-up limit Hold'em (1 hole card, bet cap 32) — also an AI training project.
- [notes on poker32](Card%20Games/Poker%20%26%20Betting/notes%20on%20poker32.md) – Design notes for Poker-32.
- [No-Limit Five-card Obama](Card%20Games/Poker%20%26%20Betting/No-Limit%20Five-card%20Obama.md) – Poker betting + climbing/shedding hybrid (2–6 players).
- [Pot-Limit Omaha (PLO)](Card%20Games/Poker%20%26%20Betting/Pot-Limit%20Omaha%20(PLO).md) – Double Board Omaha quick rulebook.
- [Horse-Betting Card Game 🐴](Card%20Games/Poker%20%26%20Betting/Horse-Betting%20Card%20Game%20🐴.md) – Race-track betting with two decks; rig the track and bet on horses.

### Climbing & Shedding

- [President - Revolution Edition 👑](Card%20Games/Climbing%20%26%20Shedding/President%20-%20Revolution%20Edition%20👑.md) – Fast-paced climbing game for 4–8 players with revolution mechanics.
- [Il Conto, Prego! 🍛](Card%20Games/Climbing%20%26%20Shedding/Il%20Conto,%20Prego!%20🍛.md) – Push-your-luck bluff & climbing for 3–8 players (declare escalating combinations, call the bill).
- [Liar's Hand 🃏](Card%20Games/Climbing%20%26%20Shedding/Liar's%20Hand%20🃏.md) – Liar's Dice with cards: 5-card hands, J/Q/K wild, escalating rank bids, call Liar! to reveal all hands.

### Push Your Luck

- [Royal Gambit 👑](Card%20Games/Push%20Your%20Luck/Royal%20Gambit%20👑.md) – Flip cards to accumulate points; bust on duplicate ranks.
- [Pirate's Plunder 🦜🗺](Card%20Games/Push%20Your%20Luck/Pirate's%20Plunder%20🦜🗺.md) – Reveal cards for treasure; spades end your turn and discard everything.

### Auction & Economic

- [Masterworks 🎨🖌](Card%20Games/Auction%20%26%20Economic/Masterworks%20🎨🖌.md) – Gallery auction game: scout paintings, bluff, trade, and maximize collection value.
- [Paintings](Card%20Games/Auction%20%26%20Economic/Paintings.md) – Early notes/draft related to Masterworks.
