
## **Tank Tactics — Core Rules**


### **Objective** 🎯

Be the **last surviving player** by the end of the game. 

---

### **Setup** 🆕

- Every player starts at a **random position** on a grid.
    
- Each player begins with:
    
    - **3 hearts** ❤️❤️❤️ 
        
    - **0 Action Points (AP)** ⚡️, but they get one at the beginning of each round

---

### **Turn Timing & Action Points** ⚡️

- Once **every day** (or whenever the players meet), each player receives **1 AP** ⚡️.
    
- AP, life, and every other info is **public**

---

### **Actions** 🔫

At any time (whenever you have enough AP), you can expend AP to perform one of these:

| Action                      | Cost |                                                                      |
| --------------------------- | ---- | -------------------------------------------------------------------- |
| **Move** 🏃                 | 1    | Move your token to an adjacent unoccupied square                     |
| **Shoot another player** 🔫 | 1    | Inflicts **1 heart damage** on the target                            |
| **Gift 1 AP** 🎁            | 1    | The target token receives +1 AP <br>(no AP gets lost!)               |
| Cure someone ❤️             | 1    | The target token (within range) receives +1 **heart**                |
| **Heal yourself** 💚        | 2    | +1 heart (up to the life cap)                                        |
| **Upgrade your tank!** 🆙   | 5    | **+1 shooting/trading range** <br>**+1 hearts cap**                  |
| Capture a token 🚩          | 5    | An OK token, adjacent to your token becomes yours, and gets +1 heart |

---

### **Range, Gifts, and Revival**🏹

- **Range** 🏹: The default **range** is 2. In general, the **range** is equal to the **hearts cap** minus one ($\text{range}=\text{hearts cap}-1$).
    
- **Gifts** 🎁: You may **gifts** any amount of **AP** ⚡️ to other players within your current **range**
    
- **KO** 😵: A player reduced to **0 hearts is “dead”** (downed):
    
    - **Stealing**🥷: Their remaining AP goes to the player who dealt the fatal blow.
        
    - Dead players **remain on the grid** (they are _not removed_). 
    
- **Revival 💚:** Dead players can be revived if another player spends 1 AP ⚡️ to **gift them a heart**; they come back with **1 heart and 0 AP**. 

---

### **Jury System**⚖️

- All dead players form a **jury**.
    
- Each player **votes** for a **tank**. That tank will receive an **extra daily +1 AP**. The vote may be changed at any time.

---

### **Item Spawns** 🎁

Once per day, a **random item** *may* spawn at a random location on the map. The first player to reach that square picks it up and gains the bonus. 

- 💚 = **Heal** +1 heart
      
- ❤️ = +1 to the **hearts cap** and hearts cap 🏹
    
- 🔋 = **Refills AP** ⚡️ up to the hearts cap

---

### **Winning** 🏆

- The game ends when only **one living player remains**.
    
- At any point, the remaining players may **agree upon an order** (1st, 2nd, 3rd, ...) and end the game. 

---

### The game in practice

The players will participate in a dedicated **Telegram chat**. We will meet **once a day**, when all the players are available. Players will submit the actions, and the *Game Master* will **execute the actions** in order while **sharing the screen** with the current game state.

At the beginning of the meeting, every live tank gets +1 AP, and the player actions are discussed by voice. 

To settle priority disputes, the last to have taken an action will have lower priority than the others. In the cases where this rule isn't enough, we shall rely on the group chat, standing by the motto: *"First come, first served!"*.
