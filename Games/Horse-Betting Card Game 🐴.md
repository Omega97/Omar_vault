
## Game Rules


### Requirements

- Two decks of 52 cards
- Tokens to keep track of the point

---

### Setup

- Take 2 decks of 52 cards each, and take 4 aces of different suits out first (they will be the **HORSES** of the **RACE**)
- One player is randomly chosen to be the **HOUSE** for the turn. The **HOUSE** is the player that "hosts the race" that turn.
- The house shuffles the deck, and deals 4 cards to each player

### The Race

It's now time to make the **HORSES** race! The **HOUSE** runs the **RACE**.

- The **HORSES** are placed on the square before the first track card
- The **HOUSE** shows the top card of the deck, and moves the **HORSE** of the same suit one step forward
- Once all **HORSES** have travelled a certain number of squares, the according **TRACE** card is revealed (ğŸ´), and the THORSE of the according suit has to move one square backward (if possible)
- The first **HORSE** to get out of the **TRACK**, wins the **RACE** (*see figure below*)
- Then, procede to the **pay-outs**, and the next player hosts the new race!
