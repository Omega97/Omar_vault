## ğŸ”„ **Gameplay**

On your turn, you may **HIT** or **STAY**.

1. **HIT**: Flip the top card of the deck.
    
    - **Apply Effects**: Resolve the card immediately (e.g., passing an 8, or a Jack forcing 2 more flips). If a Jack forces a flip that reveals _another_ Jack, the effect stacks.
        
    - **Check for Bust**: If you have two **numbered** cards of the same rank (Aâ€“10), you bust and score 0 for the turn.
    
2. **STAY**: If you are the point leader, you can decide to *stay*, end your turn, and calculate your score.

The player with **most points** **wins the hand**.

### **Going Bust ğŸ’¥**

You **Bust** the moment you have **two numbered cards of the same rank** (Ace through 10) face-up on your side of the table.

- **The Safe Cards:** Only numbered cards (Aâ€“10) cause a bust. The figuresâ€”**Jacks, Queens, and Kings**â€”are "Royal" and safe; you can collect as many of them as you like without ever risking a bust.
    
- **The Penalty:** When you bust, your turn ends immediately and you score **0 points** for the hand.
    
- **Extra Life:** The **Queen** is your only protection against disaster. If you flip a duplicate card while you have a Queen face-up, cover the Queen and the duplicate card you just flipped, and keep playing.

## ğŸ **Ending the Game**

- **Winning the HAND**: After all players *stayed* or have *busted*, the player with most points **wins the hand**.
    
- **_Cappotto_**: if everyone else *busted*, you win the hand, even with 0 points. 
    
- **Winning the GAME**: You may decide on a **set number of wins**. For example, the first player to win **3** hands, **wins the game**.
